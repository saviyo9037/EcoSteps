Ecosteps: Everyday Sustainability

Simple energy-saving actions for a better tomorrow

🏠 Home

  • 💡 Switch to LED Bulbs
  • 🔌 Unplug Idle Electronics
  • 🪟 Use Natural Light
  • 🛏️ Turn Off Lights When Not Needed
  • 🧊 Insulate Your Home

🍳 Kitchen

  • 🍽️ Use Microwave Instead of Oven
  • 🔥 Cover Pots While Cooking
  • 🧊 Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

🧺 Laundry

  • 🧼 Wash with Cold Water
  • 🌞 Line Dry Clothes
  • 🧽 Clean Dryer Lint Filter Regularly

🧑‍💻 Office / Study Room

  • 🖥️ Use Power Saving Mode
  • 🔌 Smart Power Strips

🚗 Travel

  • 🚶‍♀️ Walk or Bike Short Distances
  • 🚙 Carpool When Possible
  • 🚌 Use Public Transport

🚫 Outlier Tip

💨 Install a Rooftop Wind Turbine — While renewable, it's costly, impractical for daily life, and doesn't fit the goal of simple, everyday actions.
